What is the cooling-off period for mutual divorce (Aapsi sehmati se talaq)?

Under Section 13B of the Hindu Marriage Act, there is generally a 6-month cooling-off period between the "first motion" and "second motion". However, under certain conditions, Supreme Court guidelines allow this period to be waived by the family court. We can advise you on whether your specific case qualifies for a waiver.
