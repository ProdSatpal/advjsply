Title: Nagpur mein “Marriage By Mistake”? Annulment aur Divorce ka Simple Guide (Aur Aap Mujhse Kaise Help Le Sakte Hain)
Nagpur mein bahut log aaj kal aise phase se guzar rahe hain jahan unka “marriage by mistake” ho gaya – kabhi fraud se, kabhi pressure se, kabhi galat promises se. Log confused hote hain ki divorce lena hai ya shaadi cancel karwani hai (annulment / null & void).

Is blog mein main simple Hinglish mein samjha raha hoon ki law ise kaise dekhta hai, aapke options kya hote hain, aur aap Nagpur mein mujh tak kaise pahunch sakte hain.

1. “Married by mistake” ka matlab ground level pe kya hota hai?
Real life mein “married by mistake” kuch aise cases hote hain:

Aapko laga ye sirf documents / licence / formality hai, lekin baad mein pata chala ki marriage ho gayi.

Aapke documents kisi ne misuse karke shaadi ke papers bana diye.

Samne wali side ne important baatein chhupa di – jaise pehle se shaadi, serious illness, criminal case, ya fake identity.

Aapko dabav, dhamki, emotional blackmail se “haan” bulwa li gayi.

Aap kabhi husband–wife ki tarah rehna hi nahi chahte the, lekin ab photos, videos, documents dikhakar aapko force kiya ja raha hai.

Law ke nazdeek ye sab sirf “galti” nahi hote – in par strong legal grounds ban sakte hain.

2. Annulment vs Divorce – farak samajhna zaroori hai
Yahin pe sabse zyada confusion hota hai.

Divorce = Court maanta hai ki shaadi valid thi, lekin ab legally khatam ki ja rahi hai.

Annulment / Null & Void / Voidable Marriage = Court se request ki jaati hai ki ye shaadi pehle se hi legally theek nahi thi, isliye ise “cancel” ya “void” declare kiya jaye.

“Marriage by mistake” type matter mein kaafi baar annulment better option hota hai, lekin har case alag hota hai.
Ye decide karne ke liye aapki story, documents, religion/personal law, aur timing sab dekhna padta hai. Isi liye personalised legal advice bohot important hai.

3. Kaun se grounds pe aap case bana sakte hain?
Aapki situation ke hisaab se law aapko support kar sakta hai agar:

Aapka consent fraud ya jhooth se liya gaya ho.

Aapko force, threat, ya heavy pressure se shaadi karwai gayi ho.

Shaadi ka ceremony khud hi legally sahi na ho, ya jo centre/mandal certificate de raha hai wo authorised na ho.

Samne wala already married ho (bigamy).

Aap minor the jab shaadi hui.

Bahut log sochte hain “ye toh bas galti thi”, lekin legally wo galti aapke favour mein strong point ban sakti hai – agar sahi tarike se use ki jaye.

4. Nagpur ke logon ke typical pain points
Jo clients “married by mistake” ke saath aate hain, unke dimaag mein aam taur pe ye sawal hote hain:

Confusion – “Mujhe divorce file karna chahiye ya annulment? Kya better rahega?”

Reputation ka darr – “Ghar wale, society, relatives kya kahenge? Mera naam news mein toh nahi aa jayega?”

Samne wali side ki dhamkiyan – photos, chats, videos leak karne ki dhamki, false case ka pressure.

Process ka andhera – “Kitna time lagega? Kitna kharcha hoga? Kya mujhe abhi protection mil sakti hai?”

Ye confusion ki wajah se log action delay kar dete hain, aur kabhi‑kabhi baad mein case weak ho jata hai. Isliye early, sahi advice lena bohot zaroori hai.

5. General process – simple language mein
Har case ka exact roadmap alag hota hai, lekin broadly steps kuch aise dikhte hain:

Detailed consultation

Aap poori kahani open aur honestly batate hain – shaadi kaise hui, kya bataya gaya, kya chhupaya gaya, kya proof hai.

Hum decide karte hain ki aapke liye annulment better hai, divorce, ya dono options ko legally frame karna chahiye.

Evidence aur documents ka collection

Chats, calls, photos, videos, certificates, bank details, complaint copies, witnesses – sab identify kiya jata hai.

Shaadi ke ceremony aur papers legally valid the ya nahi, ye bhi check hota hai.

Sahi court mein sahi case file karna

Family court mein petition for annulment/nullity ya divorce.

Zarurat padne par parallel complaints ya protective remedies (fraud, cheating, domestic violence, threats).

Interim protection (jahan possible ho)

Court se relief maangna taaki harassment kam ho, misuse of documents roka ja sake, ya kuch actions pe stay mil sake.

Final order aur future planning

Court ka final decision aane ke baad aapko clear ho jata hai ki aapka legal status kya hai, aage remarriage, rights, obligations ka kya scene rahega.

Aapko procedure ya sections yaad rakhne ki zarurat nahi hai – wo mera kaam hai. Aapko bas sahi time pe sahi help leni hai.
