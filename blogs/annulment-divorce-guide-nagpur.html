"Marriage By Mistake"? Simple Guide to Annulment and Divorce in Nagpur

March 22, 2026 Adv. Jasvinder Singh Ply
4 min read

Many people in Nagpur find themselves in a distressing situation where they feel their marriage was a "mistake"—whether due to fraud, undue pressure, or hidden facts. Understanding your legal options between Annulment and Divorce is the first step toward freedom.

1. What defines "Marriage by Mistake"?

  • Fraudulent Consent: Being misled about the nature of the ceremony or documents.
  • Physical/Emotional Pressure: Being forced into a "Yes" under threats or blackmail.
  • Hidden Facts: Concealment of previous marriage, serious illness, or fake identity.
  • Misuse of Documents: Registration based on unauthorized use of your identity papers.

2. Annulment vs. Divorce: The Key Difference

Divorce ends a legally valid marriage. Annulment, however, asks the court to declare that the marriage was never legally valid in the first place (Null & Void). For many "mistake" cases, annulment is a cleaner legal route.

3. Building Your Case in Nagpur

The law supports you if you can provide evidence of fraud, bigamy (the other party is already married), or if essential legal ceremonies were never performed. Early evidence collection is critical.

4. Our 5-Step Resolution Process

  1. Confidential Consultation: We honestly evaluate your facts and evidence.
  2. Evidence Identification: Collecting chats, videos, and witnesses.
  3. Filing in Family Court: Strategically drafting the petition for maximum success.
  4. Interim Relief: Seeking court orders to stop harassment or identity misuse.
  5. Final Decree: Securing your legal status for a fresh start.

Nagpur mein Annulment vs Divorce: Kya hai antar aur Sahi Rasta

March 22, 2026 Adv. Jasvinder Singh Ply
4 min read

Nagpur mein bahut se log khud ko aisi situation mein paate hain jahan unhe lagta hai ki unki shaadi ek "galti" thi—chahe wo dhokhadhari (fraud), pressure ya facts chhupane ki wajah se ho. Annulment aur Divorce ke beech ke antar ko samajhna hi sahi kadam hai.

1. "Marriage by Mistake" kya hota hai?

  • Dhokhadhari wali Consent: Shaadi ke documents ya nature ke baare mein galat batana.
  • Pressure: Kisi dhamki ya blackmail ke dar se "Haan" kehna pada ho.
  • Pecheeda/Chhupaye gaye Facts: Pehli shaadi, bimari ya galat identity chhupana.
  • Documents ka misuse: Aapke ID cards ka galat use karke registration karna.

2. Annulment vs Divorce: Kya farq hai?

Divorce ek valid shaadi ko khatam karta hai. Lekin Annulment mein Court ye declare karti hai ki shaadi shuru se hi kanooni taur par valid nahi thi (null and void). "Galti" ke cases mein Annulment ek behtar rasta hai.

3. Nagpur mein Case kaise taiyar karein?

Agar aapke sath dhokha hua hai, ya saamne wala pehle se hi married hai, toh kanoon aapki madad karta hai. Sahi saboot (evidence) ikkattha karna sabse zaroori hai.

4. Hamara 5-Step Solution Process

  1. Confidential Consulting: Hum aapke facts aur sabooton ka analysis karte hain.
  2. Evidence Checking: Chats, videos aur gawahon ko identify karna.
  3. Family Court mein Filing: Sahi tarike se petition taiyar karna.
  4. Interim Relief: Pareshani ya identity misuse rokne ke liye Court orders lena.
  5. Final Decree: Ek nayi shuruat ke liye apna legal status secure karna.

Nyay ki ore kadam
