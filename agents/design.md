# Design System: Advocate Jasvinder Singh Ply

This design system is inspired by the professional and trustworthy aesthetic of the Advocate Jasvinder Singh Ply website. It features a sophisticated color palette, elegant typography, and a clean, structured layout suitable for legal services.

## Brand Personality
*   **Professional:** Conveying expertise and experience in the legal field.
*   **Trustworthy:** Establishing a sense of reliability and integrity.
*   **Clear:** Prioritizing readability and ease of navigation for clients.
*   **Modern yet Traditional:** Balancing contemporary design elements with the formal nature of legal practice.

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors
*   **Deep Navy Blue:** `#0a1d37` - Used for primary backgrounds, headers, and emphasis. Represents stability and authority.
*   **Gold/Amber:** `#eab308` - Used for primary call-to-action buttons and highlighting key information. Represents excellence and value.

### Secondary Colors
*   **White:** `#ffffff` - Used for main content backgrounds and text on dark backgrounds.
*   **Light Gray:** `#f3f4f6` - Used for section backgrounds and subtle borders.
*   **Dark Gray:** `#374151` - Used for secondary text and icons.

### Status Colors
*   **Success:** `#10b981` (Green)
*   **Error:** `#ef4444` (Red)
*   **Warning:** `#f59e0b` (Amber)

## Typography

### Headings
*   **Font Family:** Serif (e.g., Playfair Display or similar)
*   **Weight:** Bold
*   **Color:** Deep Navy Blue or Gold for emphasis.
*   **Usage:** Used for main page titles and section headers to create a formal and authoritative look.

### Body Text
*   **Font Family:** Sans-serif (e.g., Inter, Roboto, or similar)
*   **Weight:** Regular (400) or Medium (500)
*   **Color:** Dark Gray or Deep Navy Blue.
*   **Usage:** Used for all descriptive text, paragraphs, and lists to ensure high legibility.

## Layout & Spacing
*   **Grid System:** A standard 12-column grid for flexible and organized layouts.
*   **White Space:** Generous use of white space to prevent clutter and allow the content to breathe.
*   **Alignment:** Consistent left-alignment for text to maintain a professional and structured feel.
*   **Padding/Margins:** Standardized spacing units (e.g., 4px, 8px, 16px, 24px, 32px, 48px, 64px) to ensure consistency across the design.

## UI Components

### Buttons
*   **Primary Button:** Gold background with Deep Navy Blue text. Rounded corners.
*   **Secondary Button:** Deep Navy Blue background with White text or outlined with Deep Navy Blue border.
*   **Ghost Button:** Transparent background with Deep Navy Blue or White border and text.

### Cards
*   **Service Cards:** Simple white cards with subtle box shadows, featuring an icon, heading, and brief description.
*   **Testimonial Cards:** Styled with a distinct border or background to stand out, often including the client's name and location.

### Forms
*   **Input Fields:** Clean, outlined fields with clear labels and focused states.
*   **Submit Buttons:** Prominent primary buttons for clear call-to-action.

## Imagery
*   **Professional Portraits:** High-quality photography of legal professionals to build trust.
*   **Icons:** Simple, outlined icons related to legal services (e.g., scales of justice, gavel, documents).
*   **Background Textures:** Subtle patterns or solid deep colors to add depth without being distracting.
