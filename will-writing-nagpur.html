Service Deep-Dive

Is registration of a Will mandatory?

While not mandatory under the Registration Act, we strongly recommend it. Registering your Will in Nagpur ensures its authenticity is hard to contest in future civil litigations.

When should you update your Will?

Significant life events like marriage, divorce, or new property acquisition warrant a "Codicil" or a fresh Will to accurately represent your current status.

Vasitat (वसीयत) kyun likhni chaiye?

Nagpur mein bina Will (वसीयत) ke, property ke chakkar mein parivar aksar Courts mein barson tak uljhe rehte hain. **Indian Succession Act, 1925** ke tehat, ek legally mazboot vasiyat hi aapke rishthon aur property ki suraksha karti hai.
