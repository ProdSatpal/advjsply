Frequently Asked Questions

Notice period for Cheque Bounce

Notice must be sent within 30 days of receiving the "Memo" from the bank. The party has 15 days to pay before criminal action begins.

Notice to NMC/Govt

As per Sec 80 CPC, a 2-month notice is mandatory before suing any government body like the Nagpur Municipal Corporation (NMC).
